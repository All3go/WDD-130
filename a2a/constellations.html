Discover the Zodiac

Curious about what your zodiac sign says about you? This page explores the twelve zodiac constellations and their meanings. Whether you're deep into astrology or just getting started, you'll find insights into each sign's personality traits, symbolism, and celestial origins.

Aries

March 21 – April 19

The Ram – Bold, ambitious, and full of energy. Aries are natural leaders who love a challenge.

Taurus

April 20 – May 20

The Bull – Dependable and grounded. Taurus signs value stability, comfort, and beauty.

Gemini

May 21 – June 20

The Twins – Adaptable, curious, and witty. Geminis are social butterflies with a love for learning.

Cancer

June 21 – July 22

The Crab – Emotional, caring, and deeply intuitive. Cancers value home, family, and connection.

Leo

July 23 – August 22

The Lion – Confident, passionate, and generous. Leos love the spotlight and are natural performers.

Virgo

August 23 – September 22

The Maiden – Detail-oriented, practical, and kind. Virgos strive for perfection and love to help others.

Libra

September 23 – October 22

The Scales – Balanced, charming, and diplomatic. Libras seek harmony and are great at seeing all sides.

Scorpio

October 23 – November 21

The Scorpion – Intense, passionate, and deeply emotional. Scorpios are loyal and fiercely protective.

Sagittarius

November 22 – December 21

The Archer – Adventurous, optimistic, and philosophical. Sagittarians crave freedom and knowledge.

Capricorn

December 22 – January 19

The Goat – Ambitious, disciplined, and responsible. Capricorns are goal-driven and patient.

Aquarius

January 20 – February 18

The Water Bearer – Innovative, independent, and humanitarian. Aquarians think outside the box and love new ideas.

Pisces

February 19 – March 20

The Fish – Compassionate, imaginative, and intuitive. Pisces are dreamers with deep empathy.

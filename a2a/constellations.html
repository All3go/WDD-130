Discover the Zodiac

Curious about what your zodiac sign says about you? This page explores the twelve zodiac constellations and their meanings. Whether you're deep into astrology or just getting started, you'll find insights into each sign's personality traits, symbolism, and celestial origins. See what your sign says about you by finding the one that includes your birthdate.

Aries

March 21 – April 19

The Ram – Ruled by Mars, Aries is the fiery first sign of the zodiac, symbolizing new beginnings. Known for boldness and drive, Aries individuals blaze trails with fearless ambition, always ready to charge into action.

Taurus

April 20 – May 20

The Bull – Steady and sensual, Taurus is grounded in Earth and ruled by Venus. This sign values comfort, beauty, and loyalty. Like the stars in its constellation, Taurus shines with quiet strength and enduring presence.

Gemini

May 21 – June 20

The Twins – Symbolizing duality and communication, Gemini is ruled by Mercury. These air signs are known for their wit, curiosity, and lively spirit. They’re the storytellers of the zodiac, always chasing the next big idea.

Cancer

June 21 – July 22

The Crab – Deeply emotional and ruled by the Moon, Cancer is a sign of intuition, memory, and care. Like the stars tracing its protective shell, Cancer nurtures those they love with fierce loyalty and a soft heart.

Leo

July 23 – August 22

The Lion – Radiating solar energy, Leo is ruled by the Sun. This fire sign symbolizes courage, leadership, and creative expression. Leos are natural stars—shining bright and inspiring others with warmth and passion.

Virgo

August 23 – September 22

The Maiden – Ruled by Mercury and grounded in Earth, Virgo blends intellect with care. This sign is linked to harvest and healing, always seeking to improve and support others through thoughtful action.

Libra

September 23 – October 22

The Scales – With Venus as its ruler, Libra stands for balance, beauty, and justice. This air sign thrives on connection and fairness, constantly seeking harmony in relationships and the world around them.

Scorpio

October 23 – November 21

The Scorpion – Mysterious and magnetic, Scorpio is ruled by Pluto and Mars. Associated with transformation, this water sign dives deep into emotional truths, wielding power and passion in equal measure.

Sagittarius

November 22 – December 21

The Archer – Guided by Jupiter, Sagittarius represents adventure, wisdom, and truth-seeking. These fire signs are explorers at heart—drawn to distant horizons and cosmic questions that expand the mind.

Capricorn

December 22 – January 19

The Goat – Ambitious and steady, Capricorn is ruled by Saturn and climbs the mountain of success with patience and precision. This Earth sign is rooted in tradition, responsibility, and long-term goals.

Aquarius

January 20 – February 18

The Water Bearer – Ruled by Uranus, Aquarius channels innovation, rebellion, and intellect. This air sign brings a futuristic vision and humanitarian heart, pouring new ideas into the collective well.

Pisces

February 19 – March 20

The Fish – Dreamy and deeply intuitive, Pisces is ruled by Neptune and connected to the mystical. This water sign blurs boundaries between reality and imagination, swimming in emotion, creativity, and empathy.
